Some Easy Flute Pieces

Try to start with easy pieces first. It will allow you to get familiar with reading notes and performing the corresponding fingerings. Once you think you are familiar with the notes and fingerings, you can start on more challenging pieces. Click here to browse through and choose some easy pieces to start with.
