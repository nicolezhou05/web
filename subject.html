How to Play

You can start learning how to make a sound on a flute by using just the head joint first. Put your lips on the plate. Do not cover the hole. To get the right embouchure, you say "mmm" and then "pu". Keep blowing while turning the head joint to find your perfect spot. In this video, the teacher teaches how to make a sound by using a head joint, how the embouchure should be like, and how to tongue. You should also learn how to tune. Tuning is important because you want to know if you are playing at the right pitch. You can use an online tuner here or buy one yourself.
